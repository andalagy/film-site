About Andrew

His writing focuses on moments just before something is named—when memory, sound, and emotion are still unsettled. Dialogue circles rather than explains. Ordinary places shift quietly, time bends, and meaning arrives late, if at all. Across film and prose, his work trusts silence and ambiguity, inviting the audience to sit with what lingers rather than what resolves.
